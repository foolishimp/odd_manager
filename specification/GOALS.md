# odd_manager Goals

**Status**: Active
**Date**: 2026-04-10

## Position

Goals focus one bounded wave of work.

They are narrower than intent and shorter-lived than product definition.

They keep the current design and repricing work oriented without turning
temporary implementation choices into accidental law.

## Current Goals

- establish `odd_manager` as one forward-only control surface rather than a
  rename, inherited shell, or compatibility wrapper
- reprice the canonical control-surface ontology against the current live
  `odd_method` builder/domain truth rather than preserving older first-slice
  observer assumptions
- make ambiguity, capability gating, bounded stop states, and policy-bearing
  domain overlays first-class operator truth where the live `odd_method` line
  publishes them
- write and maintain shared design law under `build_tenants/common/design/`
  from the repriced constitutional surface rather than from stale UI precedent
- publish the retained shell, inspector, board, and graph-workspace visual
  language as `odd_manager`-owned design law while rejecting transport
  metaphor as product truth
- borrow progressive-disclosure and control-plane clarity from Paperclip while
  rejecting its company/org/task semantic model
- treat the product as pre-release and forward-only: there is no obligation to
  preserve backward compatibility for stale observer payloads, UI structures, or
  terminology that no longer match live upstream truth
- keep implementation subordinate to the repriced constitutional and shared
  design surfaces rather than letting current tenant code become accidental law
